Getting Started

Before you start submitting or analyzing meetings, please follow these instructions to ensure your meetings will work with our website.

1. Sign in to Zoom through your web browser.

2. After signing in, you'll be brought to the Meetings page. Click on "Settings" in the left hand panel.

3. Once on the settings page, click "Recording" in the navigation bar.

4. Locate the "Cloud Recording" heading. Toggle the switch next to it on if it is not already.

5. Toggle on the "Audio transcript" feature under "Advanced cloud recording settings".

*Note: Without these settings enabled, recorded meetings cannot be submitted or analyzed. You must be the meeting host and have recorded it to the cloud for a given meeting to submit successfully.
