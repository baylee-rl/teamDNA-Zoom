Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I gain access to instructor privileges?

A: Please email us at teamdna.help@gmail.com


Q: Why aren't my meetings displaying on the Submit Meetings page?

A: Please review the instructions on the 'Getting Started' page that displayed when you signed up. If this does not apply, check your Cloud Recordings in the Zoom Web Portal or contact us.


Q: Why am I not receiving any meetings as an instructor?

A: First, another user must submit meetings to your account via their submission page, using the correct email associated with your account.


Q: Why are there two instances of the same person in my analytics?

A: This can sometimes occur if someone changes their display name from one meeting to another. To avoid this, try to keep your name consistent in the meetings you attend. Also, try to ensure all participants are signed into their Zoom accounts prior to the meeting.


Q: What will happen if there are two people in my meeting with the same name?

A: As of now, if two people in the same meeting have the same display name, they will be viewed as the same person by our web app. To prevent this, please ask the participants to somehow distinguish themselves in their display names.
